Running / Jogging

Great for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.

Jump Rope

Excellent full-body workout that improves coordination and burns calories quickly.
Aim for intervals of 1-2 minutes with 30-second rest periods.

Cycling

Can be done on a stationary bike or outdoors.
Try interval training: alternate between high intensity and moderate pace.
